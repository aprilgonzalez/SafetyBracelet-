WHAT IS THE SAFETY BRACELET

The safety bracelet is an electronic bracelet that can be worn by men and women. It has a little button that can pressed whenever the person is in danger. Pressing the button sends a message to a trusted contact with the location.

How does it help?

It helps by taking steps to ensure women's physical saftey.

Does it fix the enitre problem?

This doesn't fix the entire problem. This bracelet is one of the steps we can take to ensure that women feel safe until the problem is completely solved.

Mission

Our mission as young adults is to make sure everyone is safe wherever they go. We want people to feel like they are protected and in case there's an emergency, they can simply press the button their bracelet.
